Dimensional Prision Break

My first ever game jam! This project was made in a 48h game jam organized by yoyo games to promote the game makers studio 2 that was releasing during this time.

Dimensional Prison Break is a fun local multiplayer game up to 4 players where you and your friends will have to compete against each other in order to escape the first from that prison! Choose one of the villains locked in the facility and its abilities and get ready to run and survive.

The team was composed by two artists, a game designer and me the programmer. This jam was quite a challenge because we were starting doing jams and we had to learn a whole new engine and that was just coming out.
